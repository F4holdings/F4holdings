B A Life Support System Equipments

F4 provides Life Support Systems (Breathing Systems) that are well suited for Inert, Toxic & Hazardous Work Environments. These are certified as per standards set by International Certifying Bodies. These Life Support Systems are provided with Umbilical Lines & Air Hoses for confined spaces where Oxygen is present in scarce amounts. A Low Pressure Alarm System is also installed in the Life Support System.
  • Full Body Harness
  • AGA Mask with Demand Valve and Mike
  • Tripod with Fall Arrestor
  • Air Mover Conical
  • Rope Ladder

Full Body Harness

The Full Boady Harness is safer than safety belt because it distributes the body weight uniforly and prevents the injury due to the impact of fall.

AGA Mask with Demand Valve and Mike

AGA maerks are used for breathing auxiliaryair lines in oxygen deficient atmosphere.

Tripod with Fall Arrestor

The tripod with fall aresstor setup protects the person from falling down due to any mishap while working at elevation or at large depths.

Air Mover Conical

Conical air movers, basically called as forced ventilators are used for degassing the vessel, column, tanks or any such units containing toxic gases.

Rope Ladder

Rope ladders are used in the works at elevated or deep underground places wherein a person has to ascend / descend for conducting the job.
