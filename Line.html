Line/Flow Stop Equipment

F4 Designs and Manufactures its Hot Tap/ Line Stop Fittings abiding with ASME Design Code.
We can Manufacture pipeline fittings from 2 inch to above 48 Inch depending upon clients request.
  • Sandwich Valves/Slim Valves
  • Line Stop Housing
  • Line Stop Heads
  • Line Stop Accuators
  • Manual Jack Screw
  • Sealing Element
  • Power Stop
  • Pipe Freezing Jackets

Sandwich Valves / Slim Valves

F4 Manufactures Sandwich Valves/Slim Valves which are specially designed for Hot Tapping/Line Stopping Operations with restricted heights without compromising the design parameter on its working pressure.
Manually Operated 2 Inch to 16 Inch
Hydraulically Operated 18 Inch to 48 Inch
Pressure Rating ANSI 150/300/600 (Higher Rating can be supplied against specific request or orders)

Line Stop Housing

    
Line Stop Housing to house the Line Stop Heads Assembly with the Sealing Element during the Line/Flow Stop operations with or without Bypass Branch out.
Sizes 2 Inch to 48 Inch
Type With By Pass | without by-pass
Pressure Rating ANSI 150/300/600 & PN Series (Higher Rating can be supplied against specific request or orders)

Line Stop Head

Line Stopping/Plugging Head is a device used for temporary stopping of Flow of pipeline products during the Line/Flow Stop operations on the pipelines.
Sizes 1.5 Inch to 48 Inch
Pressure Rating ANSI 150/300/600 (Higher Rating can be supplied against specific request or orders)

Line Stop Accurators



Line Stop Cylinders for Installing/Inserting the Line/Flow Stopping Head with Sealing Element in the Line/Flow Stopping Operations, in order to isolate a section of line for repairs or additions without interruption of service.
Sizes 3 Inch to 48 Inch

Manual Jack Screw

Manual Jack Screw these perform Line/Flow Stopping for sizes below 18 Inches at High/Low Pressure lines.
Sizes 3 inch to 18 inch (Above 18 inch can be manufactured & supplied based on Customer requirement)

Sealing Elements

F4 supplies Sealing Element that are ready drilled and ground to size or drilled and unground depending upon customer’s requirement
Sizes 3 Inch to 48 Inch
Type NBR, EPDM, Viton and Polyurethane material

Power Stop

Power Stop Is a Light Weight equipment that comes in sizes from 3 inch to 12 inches. The Power Stop System works to 16 bar and is factory tested to 24 bar. The Sealing Head expands 20mm, this allows the seal; to make contact inside the pipe regardless of the ovality. The Hydraulically Operated Power Stop will be provided with a Power Unit. We have also designed a cleaning tool that allows the contractor to see into the pipeline and remove any deposits before inserting the Power Stop Heads. The system also comes with a tool to remove any lost Coupons.
Manually Operated 3 inch, 4 inch, 5 inch & 6 inch
Hydraulically Operated 6 inch, 8 inch, 10 inch & 12 inches

Pipe Freezing Jackets

The Pipe Freezing Jackets are used to freeze the product inside the pipe to form a solid ice plug that allows for the modification of pipelines or Replacing of valves, without any system Shutdown /Interruption and without draining the product from the pipeline.
Size ½ inches to 24 inches NB Pipes
(Above 24 inches can be supplied against specific request or orders)/span>
MOC Aluminium / SS 304
