Bench Roller Conveyor

Bench Roller Conveyor are Cost-Effective Gravity Rollers can be used for many purposes including, transfer sections between Belt Conveyors, Live Roller Conveyors for Storing Empty or Filled Containers to Workstations for Shipping.

Features
• These are suitable for the Transportation of all kinds of Boxes, Bags, Pallets, Bulk Materials etc.

• It can Transport a Single Piece of Material with a Large Weight or Withstand a Large Impact Load.

• Simple Structure, High Reliability, Easy to use & Maintain.
