Hot Tap / Line Stop Fittings

The Tees are used when Hot Tapping or Line/Flow Stopping Operations are executed on the live pipelines. F4 Specialised in engineering, designing and manufacturing of Pipeline Fittings ,such as Hot Tap / Line Stop Tee’s, Fabricated or Forged type, Welding or Bolting type, with Closure Flange and Plugs / Flow Through Guide bar plugs, Hot Tap Branch Nozzles with RF Pads, Gas Bagging Line Stop Nozzles with Closure Flange and Plugs, 3 - way Split Tee’s.

Our Pipeline fittings are designed and manufactured to ensure the integrity of the piping system is maintained. We adopt ASME 31.1, ASME 31.4/8 codes in designing of our pipeline Fittings. We manufacture ANSI #150, #300, #600 and in PN Series up to 48 Inches with various types of Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel and Mild Steel.

Hot Tap/Line Stop Full Encirclement Split Tees

Hot Tap/Line Stop Full Encirclement Split Tees is a fitting required for hot Tapping OR Line Stopple Operation. We Supply both fabricated & forged /extruded type of Full encirclement split tees.
Sizes 2 Inch to 48 Inch
Pressure Rating 150# | 300# | 600#
(Higher Pressure Rating Avaliable Upon Special Request)

Hot Tap/Line Stop 3-Way Tees

Hot Tap/Line Stop 3-Way Tees are similar to Hot Tap/Line Stop Full Encirclement Split Tee, with an additional branch connection that provided the multi branch connections in a hot tapping operation OR Temporary/Permanent bypass in Line Stopple Operation.
Sizes 2 Inch to 48 Inch
Pressure Rating 150# | 300# | 600#
(Higher Pressure Rating Avaliable Upon Special Request)

Hot Tap/Line Stop Mechanical Tees

These Hot Tap/Line Stop Mechanical Tees are similar to Hot Tap/Line Stop Full Encirclement Split Tee in design and construction. However, Instead of welding these split tees are fitted to the mainline using fasteners on the both longitudinal sides of the split tee. These are generally recommended for low pressure operations.
Sizes 2 Inch to 48 Inch
Pressure Rating 150# & PN SERIES

Flanged Nozzle with RF Pad

Flanged Nozzle with RF Pad are used in hot tapping operation, where hot tapping size is comparatively smaller to the main line size. These fittings are of fabricated type.
Sizes 2 Inch to 48 Inch
Pressure Rating 150# | 300# | 600#
(Higher Pressure Rating Avaliable Upon Special Request)

Flanged Nozzle

Flanged Nozzle are used in hot tapping operation, where the Hot Tapping size is comparatively smaller to mainline size and the Design pressure of pipelines is very less. These fittings are of fabricated type.

Weld-o-let Flange

Weld-O-Let Flange are used for hot tapping operations where hot Tapping size is much smaller in comparison to mainline size, but the design pressure of mainline is high.
Sizes 2 Inch to 48 Inch

Thread-O-Ring Fitting

Thread-O-Ring Fitting are special type of fittings that are an essential part of any line stopple operation, which felicitates the pressure balancing in mainline and Line Stop Operation System, and also facilitates the Draining/Venting of the product from the isolated section.
Sizes 2 Inch to 48 Inch

Flow Throuh Guide Bars

Flow-Through Guide Bars are used in combination with any type of Hot Tapping fittings, in mainline those are subject to pigging operation. These guide bars act as PIG Retainers and help in smooth pigging operation on any pipeline, post hot tapping operation.
Sizes 2 Inch to 48 Inch
Completion/Closure Plugs are an essential part of a Line Stopple fitting, which enables the closure of hot tapped opening and facilitate the smooth removal of Sandwich valve in a line stop operation. These plugs are used along with an O-Rings that acts as the primary seal to stop the flow of line product during the removal of sandwich valve. These fittings are always backed up with a permanent Blind Flange. Completion/Closure plugs can also be used in combination with Scarfed Nipples for Piggable Pipelines.
We manufacture two types of Completion Plugs. Conventional Lock-O-Ring Plugs and advanced Cam-Lock® Plugs

Camlock Plug

Sizes 4 Inch to 20 Inch (can be provided with Scarffed Nipple)

L-O-R Plug

Sizes 4 Inch to 48 Inch (can be provided with Scarffed Nipple)
